Our Products

FRESH SEMI-HUSKED COCONUT

We are one of the topmost fresh coconut exporters in India. We supply fresh, semi-husked coconuts. Our semi-husked coconuts can be easily broken and are enriched with coconut water. The coconuts are fully mature and light brown in colour. While packing the coconuts, we make sure that the fresh coconuts remain intact.

We ensure that our coconut and coconut products are not only wrapped and packed with the high class packaging materials, but also retain their freshness, taste, aroma and nutrient content while being transported to different parts of the world. This makes our products travel friendly even to long distances.

ORGANIC AND FRESH MANGOES

We are one of the prominent mango exporter from India. With our extensive experience in the food industry and our expertise in the field, we believe in providing premium quality products to our clients residing in every corner of the world.

Mangoes are plucked and stored in warehouses to ensure that we have a continuous supply of this seasonal fruit. We offer this mango in various packaging options and ensure to deliver in a promised time frame.
